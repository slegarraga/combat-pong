# Design Context

## Target audience

- Players arriving from social feeds, Discord links, or quick work-break sessions.
- Mostly mobile and laptop users who want something instantly playable and immediately competitive.
- People who enjoy the feeling of a tense 1v1 duel, but do not want accounts, setup, or waiting in a queue.

## Use cases

- Open the game and start a match in seconds.
- Feel like you are facing a live anonymous rival, even though the entire experience is local and frictionless.
- Chase rematches because the rebounds, captures, momentum swings, and end-of-match payoff feel unusually satisfying.

## Brand personality

- Underground arcade broadcast.
- Fast, sharp, premium, anonymous, and slightly theatrical.
- Ember versus ice instead of generic neon chaos.

## Product constraints

- No accounts.
- No Supabase.
- No real multiplayer.
- Must work well on mouse and touch.
- Motion should add tension and clarity, not noise.
